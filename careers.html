01

Junior Architect

Full-time · On-site · Carmona, Cavite
Job Duties
  • Lead and develop projects from early concept through design development.
  • Prepare drawings, specifications, and construction documents.
  • Design and document commercial and industrial building projects.
  • Consult with clients to determine their requirements.
  • Coordinate preliminary architectural studies for new structures and alterations.
  • Organize and manage permit documents.
  • Resolve complex design issues with innovative, practical solutions.
  • Work with cross-functional teams and coordinate with subcontractors.
Skills & Qualifications
  • Licensed Architect.
  • 3 years' experience in the construction industry.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Knowledgeable in applicable building codes.
  • Able to produce 2D and 3D design drawings for presentations.
  • Willing to work beyond normal hours as projects require.
Salary Range: Negotiable

Civil Engineer

Full-time · On-site · Carmona, Cavite
Job Duties
  • Undertake physical and feasibility studies, including site investigations.
  • Use a range of software to develop detailed designs.
  • Perform complex calculations.
  • Liaise with clients, architects, and subcontractors.
  • Compile job specs and supervise tendering procedures.
  • Resolve design and development problems.
  • Manage budgets and project resources; schedule materials and deliveries.
  • Ensure legal, health, and safety compliance and on-time, on-budget delivery.
Skills & Qualifications
  • Licensed Civil Engineer.
  • 3 years' experience in the construction industry.
  • Able to think methodically and manage projects.
  • Excellent communication skills.
  • Negotiating, supervisory, and leadership skills.
  • Able to keep overview while attending to detailed technicalities.
