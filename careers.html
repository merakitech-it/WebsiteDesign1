Machinist — CNC Milling Operator

Full-time · On-site · Carmona, Cavite
Skills & Qualifications
  • At least a High School graduate; vocational/technical course an advantage.
  • 2–4 years' experience operating CNC milling machines (Haas, Fanuc, or equivalent).
  • Ability to read and interpret engineering drawings and GD&T.
  • Familiarity with cutting tools, offsets, and basic G-code.
About The Role
  • Set up and operate CNC milling machines (VMC/HMC) to produce precision components.
  • Perform in-process inspection using calipers, micrometers, and gauges.
  • Work within a 24/7 production environment.
  • Uphold quality standards under our ISO 9001:2015 system.
Salary Range: Negotiable

Machinist — Surface Grinding Operator

Full-time · On-site · Carmona, Cavite
Skills & Qualifications
  • At least a High School graduate; vocational/technical course an advantage.
  • 2–4 years' experience operating precision surface grinders.
  • Knowledge of wheel dressing, magnetic chucks, and coolant systems.
  • Ability to read and interpret engineering drawings and tolerances.
About The Role
  • Set up and operate surface grinding machines (Okamoto or equivalent) to achieve tight flatness and parallelism tolerances.
  • Perform in-process inspection using micrometers, height gauges, and surface plates.

  • Uphold quality standards under our ISO 9001:2015 system.
