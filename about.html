Our Story

From 25 To 70+
In One Year.

Meraki-TECH Precision Corporation, a 100% Filipino-owned company, was founded in response to a growing market in the manufacturing industry. The company was established on February 13, 2017 with ₱10,000,000 paid-up capital. Its first establishment was located at RD3 Compound, Brgy. Bancal, Carmona, Cavite — with only 25 employees.

The company met its target in its first year and maintained successful operations the next. Responding to clients' growing demand, it moved to a much larger facility in late 2018. November 23, 2018 marked official operations at the new Mountview Phase II facility in Carmona, Cavite. By December 2018, official manpower had reached 73.

Expansion & New Office
