What We Do

Core Capabilities

A complete engineering and machining sourcing solution under one roof — from a single prototype to full mass production.

01

Precision Machining & Toolmaking

CNC turning, milling, and grinding with tight-tolerance finishing for tools, dies, and precision components.

02

Jigs & Fixtures

Design, assembly, and fabrication of jigs and fixtures engineered for repeatability and throughput.

03

Tool & Die Fabrication

New build and repair of molds and dies — stamping dies, electrodes, and inspection mold parts.

04

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Bending, forming, and assembly of sheet metal parts with our metal-fabrication and bending lines.

05

Laser Cutting

High-accuracy laser cutting (KRASS) for clean, complex profiles in sheet and plate stock.

06

Mass Production

Scalable component manufacturing and assembly with consistent quality at volume.

Who We Are

Meraki — Soul Put
Into The Work.

The word Meraki is Greek: to do something with soul, creativity, and love — to put something of yourself into your work. That ethos defines how we machine every part.

Founded on February 13, 2017 as a 100% Filipino-owned corporation, Meraki-TECH has grown from 25 to 100+ employees and a purpose-built facility in Mountview Industrial Complex, Carmona, Cavite.

  • Founded & manned by committed engineering professionals
