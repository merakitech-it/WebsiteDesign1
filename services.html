Our Services

What We Deliver

We cater to varied engineering and manufacturing requirements across the market.

01

Precision Machining & Toolmaking

CNC turning & milling, surface grinding, EDM, and wire-cut EDM for tight-tolerance parts, electrodes, and tooling.

02

Design & Fabrication of Jigs & Fixtures

Custom-engineered jigs and fixtures designed, assembled, and fabricated for repeatability and production throughput.

03

Assembly & Components Manufacturing

Sub-assembly and component manufacturing — including harness components and engineering plastics.

04

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Bending, forming, and assembly of sheet metal parts using our metal-fabrication and bending lines.

05

Mold Components, Tool & Die

New build and repair of molds and dies, stamping dies, spot electrodes, and inspection mold parts.

06

Laser Cutting & Reverse Engineering

High-accuracy laser cutting for complex profiles, plus reverse engineering and manufacturing.
